Text

Body copy is always Open Sans Regular 15px with 22px of line height in Gray #3f3f3f.

Do Use an appropriate mixture of uppercase titles and lowercase copy throughout a design to add subtle variations in typography.

Don't USE ALL UPPERCASE FOR EXTREMELY LONG HEADINGS OR AS BODY COPY.

Narrative Text

Narrative copy is used when there is a longer passage of reading in the content such as in the reader. Narrative copy uses Cordale starting at 18px with 34px of line height in Gray #3f3f3f.
